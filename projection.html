SS Strategy Rules of Thumb
→ Claim at 62 if: in poor health, need income early, or have high investment returns that beat delay credits
→ Claim at 67 (FRA) if: average health, balanced portfolio, want the "no-regret" option
→ Delay to 70 if: in good health, long family history, can bridge gap with portfolio. Every year past 67 adds 8%
→ Breakeven between 67 and 70 is typically around age 82–84
